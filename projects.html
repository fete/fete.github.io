My Responsive Website Sleeper Tips

Control the Viewport: The viewport is a user’s visible area of a web page. Thus, it will change depending on the device someone uses to accesses your website

The meta “viewport” element gives the browser instructions on how to control the page’s scale and dimension. The “width=device-width” sets the width of the page to adjust to the width of the screen of the device (whether mobile or desktop). The “initial-scale=1.0” sets the zoom level of the page once it renders by the browser, on the respective device’s screen.
