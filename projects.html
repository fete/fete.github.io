Some of My Fav Products I Launched

Product Management has surely kept me engaged as I love interacting with a product and the team from all the various points of its lifecycle and beyond. Here's some clues as to what I've been up to in my career.

Landing Page Redesign

This was my first product I got to work on as a PM.The home page wasn't user friendly and needed UI/UX help, which will in turn improve the funnel metrics.
I implemented a roadmap, conducted and gathered lots of user feedback and data, worked with designers and engineers to make the new and improved landing page.

Role:
Product Manager

Improve UI/UX of Profile Page

The profile page was cluttered and wasn't providing a good UX/UI. I was tasked with making the profile a better experience for our users.
This project took a lot of research upfront with our users. I employed A/B and multivariate testing, as well as worked closely with our designers and engineers to execute the results from the gathered feedback. A key point was to also work with the marketing & customer support team to ensure the new design got great visibility and support.

Role:
Product Manager

Mobile App Profile Page

Led the team to design the profile page and a gifts page for the app. We aimed to provide a smooth transition of the site to a mobile platform so users can still recognize the site but at the same time benefit from the mobile functionality.
This project allowed me to gain a lot of knowledge of the mobile platform technologies and the different considerations one must have when building for mobile devices.

Role:
Product Manager

View & Create Orders

A way to view orders and to place them was needed. I led a team to create this product from the ground up.
